Introduction

Welcome to the IdleTron Wiki! Here you'll find detailed information about all the components of the game, including producers, managers, and boosts. Use this guide to enhance your understanding and strategy as you play IdleTron.

Producers

Hacker Laptop

Hacker Laptop

The Hacker Laptop is the first producer available to players. It generates a basic revenue of 1 coin per second. This producer is cost-effective and provides a solid foundation for building your coin generation strategy. It is crucial to invest in Hacker Laptops early in the game to maximize your income potential.

Cost: 4 coins

Revenue: 1 coin/s

Time Delay: 1 second

PC Miner

PC Miner

PC Miner is a more advanced producer that significantly boosts your coin generation capabilities. It generates 20 coins every 3 seconds, making it a valuable addition to your production lineup. Investing in PC Miners is essential for progressing in the game.

Cost: 60 coins

Revenue: 20 coins/3s

Time Delay: 3 seconds

GPU Setup

GPU Setup

The GPU Setup is one of the most powerful producers in IdleTron. It generates 90 coins every 6 seconds, providing a substantial boost to your income. GPUs are critical for advanced players looking to optimize their revenue streams.

Cost: 720 coins

Revenue: 90 coins/6s

Time Delay: 6 seconds

Cloud Mining

Cloud Mining

Cloud Mining provides a flexible and powerful method of generating coins. It earns you 250 coins every 10 seconds, allowing you to further expand your operations. This producer is ideal for scaling your income rapidly.

Cost: 5000 coins

Revenue: 250 coins/10s

Time Delay: 10 seconds

Warehouse

Warehouse

The Warehouse is an essential producer for players looking to maximize their coin production. It generates 1000 coins every 30 seconds. Investing in a Warehouse is a crucial step for serious players aiming for long-term success.

Cost: 20,000 coins

Revenue: 1000 coins/30s

Time Delay: 30 seconds

AI Trader

AI Trader

The AI Trader is a sophisticated producer that allows players to generate significant income quickly. It produces 5000 coins every minute, making it a top-tier option for serious players.

Cost: 100,000 coins

Facility

Facility

Metaverse

Metaverse

VR Station

Quantum Miner

Managers

Boosts

1 Hour Boost

1 Hour Boost

The 1 Hour Boost temporarily increases your coin generation rate for one hour, significantly enhancing your earnings during critical periods.

4 Hour Boost

4 Hour Boost

The 4 Hour Boost offers extended temporary enhancements to your coin generation, ideal for maximizing profits over a longer timeframe.

1 Day Boost

1 Day Boost

The 1 Day Boost provides a full day's enhancement to your coin production, allowing for significant strategic gains in your gameplay.

Gameplay Mechanics

In IdleTron, you will manage producers and their respective revenues to earn coins passively. Invest in managers to automate your production and utilize boosts strategically to maximize your earnings during the game. As you progress, focus on reinvesting your earnings into higher-tier producers and managers to scale your income effectively.

Conclusion

IdleTron offers an engaging and strategic gaming experience through its idle mechanics combined with producer management. Explore, upgrade, and boost your way to success in the dynamic world of IdleTron!
